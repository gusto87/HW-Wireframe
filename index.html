About Me

My name is William Sherrill. Originally from SC I moved to Charlotte NC 7 years ago. I was in construction upon moving here but, did not enjoy my work and wanted to do something I felt more meaningful. I alway was intrigued by computers and have awlays been involved with them recreationally but now I want to make this a lucrative, exciting, and worthwhile career. I am eager to learn this and grateful for this opportunity. I changed my career path to something I would feel passionate about. I relish in the thoughts of being able to create applications or interfaces that people would be able to use and benefit from. I want to make the most of this course so I aim to apply myself as much as possible. I have to learn how to not overthink things and look at them not so critically sometimes. I know that this is something I want to do and will feel a sense of accomplishment with development. I'm always open to constructive critisism and sound advice to become a prominent developer.
